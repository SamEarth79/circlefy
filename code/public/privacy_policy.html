Privacy Policy

Circlefy is an app powered by the Spotify API. By choosing to use this app, you agree to the use of your Spotify account username and data for your top artists.

None of the data used by Circlefy is stored or collected and is not shared with any third parties. All information is used solely for displaying it to you.
